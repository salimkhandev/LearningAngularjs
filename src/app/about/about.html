Mood Calculator — About

A simple calculator that gives motivational or funny messages after each calculation. ✨

What it does

Mood Calculator performs normal math operations (add, subtract, multiply, divide). After every result, it shows a short motivational or funny message — making small tasks enjoyable and cheerful. 😄

Examples

2 + 2 → 4
Message: “That’s 4! You’re as sharp as a laser 😎”
10 × 10 → 100
Message: “100! You’re multiplying success 💪”

Why this is useful

  • Turns a small task into a tiny happy moment. 😊
  • Great for learners and kids — makes practice fun. 👦👧
  • Simple UI, low distraction, quick to use. ⚡

Core Principles

⚙️ Simple: One screen, basic operations only.
💬 Friendly: Short, positive, and fun messages.
🔒 Private: Works fully offline — no data leaves your device.
Made with care — small ideas, big smiles. 😌
